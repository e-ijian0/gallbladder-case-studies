# Case Study Template

Use this template to create new case studies in this repository. Each case study should follow the structure below.

---

## **Patient Presentation**
- Age, sex, chief complaint
- Vital signs (if relevant)
- Physical exam findings pertinent to the case

## **Clinical History**
- Relevant past medical history
- Medications
- Surgical history
- Social history (smoking, alcohol, etc.)
- Family history

## **Imaging Findings**
- Modalities used (ultrasound, CT, MRI, etc.)
- Key findings with descriptions
- Images (optional, can be referenced)

## **Laboratory Findings**
- Relevant blood tests, cultures, etc.

## **Differential Diagnosis**
- List of possible diagnoses, ranked by likelihood
- Reasoning for inclusion/exclusion

## **Pathology**
- Gross and microscopic findings (if applicable)
- Histopathological description
- Immunohistochemistry (if relevant)

## **Management**
- Initial management (medical, surgical, interventional)
- Follow-up plan
- Outcomes

## **Discussion**
- Summary of the case
- Teaching points
- Key takeaways for learners
- References (optional)

---

**Note:** Keep patient information fictional and HIPAA-compliant. Ensure imaging descriptions are accurate and educational.
